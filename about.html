About Dirt Bikes USA

A passion for racing. A commitment to excellence. Since 1996.

Our Story

Dirt Bikes USA was founded in 1996 by Carl Schmidt and Steven McFadden, two young but experienced bikers with engineering backgrounds who saw the growing popularity of dirt bikes in the United States.

They developed custom frames for dirt bikes that were more suited to off-road handling and started building their own models using top-quality motorcycle engines from manufacturers like Honda and Rotax Motors of Austria.

Riding on one of their customized dirt bikes, Steven finished first in the famous Barstow to Las Vegas race — sparking so much interest that Carl and Steve decided to open a full production facility in Carbondale, Colorado.

What started in a small workshop has since expanded to a facility housing 140+ employees involved in production, design, engineering, sales, and administration.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Dirt Bikes USA story

1996

The Beginning

Carl Schmidt and Steven McFadden found Dirt Bikes USA in Carbondale, Colorado, building custom off-road motorcycle frames.

1997

First Major Win

Steven McFadden finishes first in the Barstow to Las Vegas race on a Dirt Bikes USA custom build, putting the brand on the map.

2000s

Production Expansion

The Carbondale facility expands significantly. Dirt Bikes launches the Enduro and Moto product lines, now offering four distinct models.

2010s

National Distribution

Dirt Bikes grows to a network of 40+ authorized distributors across the Western and Midwestern United States, with international sales in Europe.

Today

Racing Heritage Continues

Over 9,000 bikes sold annually with $60M+ in revenue. Models continue to place first at Daytona Bike Week and major dirt bike competitions nationwide.

Our Values

What drives everything we do at Dirt Bikes USA

Quality

We use the best components from around the world — Rotax engines from Austria, Dunlop tires, Brembo brakes, and US-made parts — to ensure every bike meets the highest standards.

Innovation

Our 3 full-time product designers and 3 engineers continually push the boundaries with modern technology including electric starters, steering stabilizers, and liquid cooling systems.

Customer Satisfaction

Our 10-person service department handles warranties and customer needs. We maintain a family atmosphere and encourage employees, distributors, and customers to share ideas.
