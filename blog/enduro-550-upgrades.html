New Enduro 550 Upgrades: Enhanced Suspension and Cooling

We're excited to announce a major upgrade package for the Enduro 550 — our flagship endurance racer. The 2024 refresh delivers meaningful improvements to the two systems that matter most in long-distance off-road competition: suspension and thermal management.

Redesigned Liquid Cooling System

The Enduro 550's Rotax engine has always been a powerhouse, but extended runs through Colorado's high-altitude trails can push any cooling system to its limits. Our engineers spent months analyzing thermal data from race conditions and developed a redesigned radiator with 18% more surface area, paired with a higher-flow water pump.

The result is a cooling system that maintains optimal engine temperature even during extended hill climbs at altitude — a critical advantage for riders competing in events like the Leadville 100 or the grueling stages of the Colorado 500. During testing on Hagerman Pass outside of Leadville, the new system kept coolant temperatures a full 15 degrees Fahrenheit lower than the previous generation under identical conditions.

Next-Generation Shock Absorbers

Working with our US-based suspension partners, we've developed a new rear shock that offers 15% more wheel travel than the outgoing unit. But the real story isn't just about travel — it's about how that travel is managed.

The new shock features a progressive damping curve that provides a plush feel over small rocks and roots, then firms up progressively as impacts get bigger. For riders, this means less fatigue over long distances and more confidence when the trail throws unexpected obstacles at you. The front forks have received matching updates to ensure balanced handling front to rear.

Additional Updates

  • Steering Stabilizer: Refined valving for improved low-speed maneuverability without sacrificing high-speed stability
  • Seat Foam: New dual-density foam that stays comfortable through all-day rides while providing better grip during aggressive riding
  • Handguards: Wider wrap-around design that offers better protection from branches and debris on tight single-track
  • Graphics: Updated colorway that reflects our 2024 racing livery

Availability and Pricing

The upgraded Enduro 550 is available now through all authorized Dirt Bikes USA dealers at the same $8,800 retail price. Existing Enduro 550 owners can purchase the suspension and cooling upgrades as a retrofit kit through the parts department — contact your local dealer or reach out to our service team for details.

The Enduro 550 has always been our answer to the question "what would the perfect endurance bike look like?" With these updates, we think we're closer than ever to that ideal. Come ride one and see for yourself.
