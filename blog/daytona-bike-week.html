Dirt Bikes USA at Daytona Bike Week 2024

Daytona Bike Week is the biggest motorcycle gathering in the United States, and this year Dirt Bikes USA is going bigger than ever. From March 1st through the 10th, you'll find our team at Booth 247 in the Main Exhibition Hall with all four of our current models on display.

What to Expect at Our Booth

We're bringing the full lineup to Daytona this year. All four models — the Enduro 250, Enduro 550, Moto 300, and the Moto 450 — will be on the show floor. Each bike will be set up on a display stand so you can get up close and examine the details that set Dirt Bikes USA apart: our signature hand-shaped frames, the Rotax engine platform, Brembo and Nissin brake components, and the Dunlop tire package.

Our product designers and engineers will be on hand to answer your questions about the bikes, our manufacturing process, and the technology behind our racing success. This is a rare chance to talk directly with the people who design and build your ride.

Meet the Racing Team

Several members of the Dirt Bikes USA factory racing team will be at the booth throughout the week for meet-and-greet sessions. Come hear first-hand stories from the trail, get tips on bike setup for your riding style, and maybe even score an autograph. Here's the schedule:

  • Saturday, March 2 (11am-1pm): Marcus Webb, 2024 National Enduro champion
  • Monday, March 4 (2pm-4pm): Kyle Jennings and Rachel Torres, Enduro Pro team riders
  • Thursday, March 7 (10am-12pm): Team manager Dave Kowalski — Q&A session on race strategy and bike preparation
  • Saturday, March 9 (12pm-3pm): Full team signing session and 2025 preview announcement

Test Ride the Moto 450

For the first time at Daytona, we're offering demo rides on the Moto 450. Our enclosed test track just outside the exhibition hall gives you a chance to feel the power of the Rotax 450cc engine and the precision of the Brembo brake system for yourself. Rides are free but slots are limited — sign up at the booth early to secure your time.

Exclusive Event Pricing

Daytona attendees who visit our booth will receive exclusive pricing on parts and accessories — up to 20% off select items including brake components, exhaust systems, and Dunlop tire packages. We'll also be offering special financing rates on new bike purchases made through our dealer network during Bike Week.

Interested in Becoming a Dealer?

Dirt Bikes USA is actively expanding our network of authorized distributors, particularly in the Southeast and Northeast regions. If you're a motorcycle dealer interested in carrying our brand, Daytona is the perfect time to start that conversation. Our sales team will have dealer information packets available and can walk you through the application process on-site.

We can't wait to see you in Daytona. Stop by Booth 247 and say hello — we'll be the ones covered in dirt.
