Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Dirt Bikes USA

Purchasing

Dirt Bikes USA motorcycles are sold exclusively through our network of 40+ authorized distributors across the United States and Europe. Visit our Dealer Locator to find the nearest authorized dealer. All motorcycle and spare parts sales, shipping, and set-up must be handled by a certified dealer.

If you live more than 50 miles from the nearest authorized Dirt Bikes USA dealer, you can purchase a motorcycle or parts through a certified independent motorcycle dealer. Retail customers can also purchase spare parts directly from Dirt Bikes USA by verifying their distance from the nearest authorized dealer. Contact us for assistance.

Our current retail prices are: Enduro 250 at $6,250, Enduro 550 at $8,800, Moto 300 at $7,295, and Moto 450 at $9,395. Visit our Products page for full specifications and details on each model.

Financing options are available through our authorized dealer network. Each dealer works with their own lending partners to offer competitive rates. Contact your nearest dealer for specific financing terms and current promotions.

Service & Warranty

Every new Dirt Bikes USA motorcycle comes with a comprehensive manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty service must be performed by an authorized Dirt Bikes dealer. Our 10-person service department at headquarters in Carbondale supports all warranty claims and works closely with dealers to resolve issues quickly.

All service and warranty repairs must be handled by an authorized Dirt Bikes USA dealer. Our dealers have factory-trained technicians familiar with our Rotax engines, Brembo/Nissin brake systems, and proprietary frame designs. Find your nearest service location on our Dealer Locator page.

Replacement parts are available through any authorized Dirt Bikes USA dealer. Our 4-person parts department maintains inventory of all components including Rotax engine parts, Brembo and Nissin brake components, Dunlop tires, exhaust systems, and more. Most common parts ship within 1-2 business days. If you live more than 50 miles from a dealer, you may order parts directly — contact our service team for details.

Products & Technology

Our Enduro models (250 and 550) are designed for cross-country endurance racing and trail riding. They feature longer suspension travel, larger fuel tanks, and softer power delivery for all-day comfort. Our Moto models (300 and 450) are purpose-built for motocross — enclosed dirt course racing with jumps, berms, and tight corners. They have sharper throttle response, firmer suspension, and lighter weight for explosive acceleration.

All Dirt Bikes USA motorcycles are powered by Rotax engines from Austria. Rotax has over 100 years of powertrain engineering experience. Our engines feature liquid cooling, and both kick and electric starters across all models. Engine sizes range from 250cc to 550cc depending on the model.

We recommend the Enduro 250 for most beginners. It's our lightest model with forgiving power delivery, making it easy to control while you build confidence. It has plenty of capability for trails and even entry-level competition. Check out our Trail Riding Tips for Beginners blog post for more guidance.

Community & Events

The Dirt Bikes USA Owners' Group is free for all Dirt Bikes USA motorcycle owners. Members get invitations to group rides, exclusive product previews, parts discounts, and a quarterly newsletter. Sign up through the contact form or ask your authorized dealer to register you.

We're always looking to expand our dealer network, particularly in the Southeast and Northeast regions. If you're a motorcycle dealer interested in carrying Dirt Bikes USA, contact our sales team to request a dealer information packet and begin the application process.
