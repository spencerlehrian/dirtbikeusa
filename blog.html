2024 Racing Season Recap: Dirt Bikes USA Dominates Enduro Circuit

It's been an incredible year for the Dirt Bikes USA racing team. Our riders brought home multiple first-place finishes across the national enduro circuit, including a thrilling victory at the legendary Barstow to Las Vegas race — continuing the tradition started by our co-founder Steven McFadden nearly three decades ago.

The Enduro 550 proved to be the bike of choice for competitive riders this season, with its Rotax engine delivering unmatched reliability over grueling multi-day races. Our engineering team's investment in steering stabilizer technology paid off with improved handling scores across all terrain types.
